Farmers markets

This presentation will look at the distribution of farmer's markets across NYC. it will first look at the ratio of median income to number of farmers market by borough. then it will look at those ratios indivisual zip codes. this is all to determine weather there is a wealth desparity when it comes to acess of healthy foods.

Data pipeLine: my data pipline was difficult due to the nyc open data being riddled with inaccuracies. this meant i could not use the districts nor find them. the data did however have accurate adresses for the farmers markets so i used those adress to find the zip codes. i then manually put the zip codes into a dictionary. to find the income i used NYC data and simple maps.com. i then used a python file to parse all of the data into smaller dictionaries to be worked with.
FeedBack: My major feedback was how i was going to display the Wealth quanity and zipcodes all on one graph. This was a concern both leehan and yanis came up with and what i had settled on was using a ratio instead of raw numbers. in this way my actual page deviates from my drawn model.
